The biggest change: you'll need an ID with your current address.

Voter registration cards don't count.

Unlike last time, you can't show up with what Elections Canada sent you to identify yourself.

Driver's Licenses and IDs do, but...

Unless you rolled out to the DMV immediately after your last move, this probably doesn't have your current address.

Thankfully, There are alternatives...

We've done the research, and found the most easily-obtained IDs with address. These include your lease, electricity / telecomm bills, and bank statements.
